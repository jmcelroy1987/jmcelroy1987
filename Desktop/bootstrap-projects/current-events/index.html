The Murder Trial of Amanda Knox

You are about to read current media articles about how an Italian court has reinstated the guilty verdict of Amanda Knox, a U.S. citizen accused of a 2007 murder in Italy. Depending on the outcome of Knox's remaining appeals, the U.S. may have to extradite her.

While you are reading, think about:

  • What is extradition? Where is it outlined in U.S. law?
  • Why do countries sign extradition treaties with one another?
  • Why do you think Knox's case has attracted so much attention?
